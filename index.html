WHO WAS DOOM?

A UNIQUE VILLAIN

MF DOOM is considered one of the most celebrated and influential musicians in hip hop. Thom Yorke, who collaborated with MF DOOM, wrote: "He was a massive inspiration to so many of us, changed things... For me the way he put words was often shocking in its genius." Another rapper, Stereogum, noted MF DOOM’s "formative" influence on younger rappers.

EVIL GENIUS: HOLORIMES

DOOM is unique because of his consistent use of multisyllabic rhyming phrases. "DOOM designed his songs to be a cascade of rhymes internally, and used external rhyme to connect lyrics.
Part of the charm was DOOM’s use of the ‘Holorime’ or an entire line or punchline that rhymes internally or externally within the bar.

DOOM's DISCOGRAPHY

OPERATION: DOOMSDAY

1998

MF DOOM’s return to rap started with a bang, with a primarily self produced album, Operation Doomsday.
It features dense rhyme schemes over tracks composed from a collage of R&B and cartoon samples.
